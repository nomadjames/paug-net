We're building something here.

PAUG started as a simple idea: get Pittsburgh's audio people in the same room. Producers, sound designers, musicians, engineers — anyone who thinks in waveforms.

The blog is about to grow. Here's what's coming:

- **Deep dives on synthesis** — wavetable, FM, granular, modular. Theory and practice.
- **Production breakdowns** — how tracks get made, from sketch to master.
- **Pittsburgh scene coverage** — studios, venues, and the people making noise in this city.
- **Tool reviews** — DAWs, plugins, hardware. Honest takes, no sponsored content.
- **Community spotlights** — featuring PAUG members and their work.

If you've got something to say, we want to hear it. Hit us up on the [contact page](/contact) to pitch a post or just say what's up.

Stay tuned.

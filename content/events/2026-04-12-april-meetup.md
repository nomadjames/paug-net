We're tearing apart Wavetable and Drift this month. Bring your laptop, headphones, and weird patches.

We'll do live sound design rounds — everyone gets 10 minutes to build something from scratch while the room watches. No presets allowed.

## What to bring
- Laptop with Ableton Live (any version)
- Headphones
- Your most cursed rack/patch (optional but encouraged)

## Format
- 2:00 - 2:30: Hang, set up, catch up
- 2:30 - 3:30: Sound design rounds
- 3:30 - 4:00: Break + listening session
- 4:00 - 5:00: Open jam / collaboration time

All skill levels welcome. If you've never opened Wavetable before, even better — fresh ears make the best sounds.
